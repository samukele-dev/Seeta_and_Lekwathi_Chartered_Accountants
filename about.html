Meet the team behind S&L

Daniel Seeta

dseeta@snlca.com

CEO Accounting Division

Daniel is a qualified Chartered Accountant with extensive experience in auditing, tax and accounting. He completed his articles with one of the Big Four international audit firms with audit clients across different industries. From this experience, he received initial training on some management, discipline, work ethic, as well as team management. Furthermore, client or stakeholder management was a pivotal element of some of his responsibilities. Upon completion of his articles, he went on to occupy managerial roles in the telecommunications and insurance industries across large and medium sized entities. In these roles he managed large teams, compiled financial statements, prepared and submitted VAT returns, taxation calculations, designed finance operational processes and internal control review, maintenance, and improvements. Having also worked for smaller entities, he had the privilege of setting up the entire finance process: from process descriptions, chart of accounts set up, ERP implementation right through to the preparation of annual financial statements. This also included putting in place internal financial controls and processes to ensure the integrity of financial data and the processing of transactions.
